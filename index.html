Privacy Policy for Aura Wall Calendar

Last Updated: 12/29/2025

1. Data Ownership & Storage

Aura is a private family utility. Your data (calendar events and board notes) is stored securely using your own private Supabase Infrastructure. We do not sell, share, or have any access to your family data.

2. System Integrations

Aura requests access to your iOS System Calendars (EventKit) locally on your device to provide a unified household dashboard. This data never leaves your private cloud instance.

3. Local Storage

Minor configuration settings (brightness, family name) are stored locally on your device using encrypted storage.

4. Contact

For support or data deletion requests, please contact your household administrator.
